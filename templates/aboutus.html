{%block subtitle%}HearingSIN-Test Online: About Us{%endblock%}






The HearingSIN-Test (or "Hearing Speech in Noise Test") provides an estimate of signal-to-noise (SNR) ratio loss. A list of sentences are presented in babble noise. The sentences are presented at SNR ratios which decrease in 5-dB steps from 25 (very easy) to 0 (very difficult). These SNR ratios range from normal to severely impaired performance in noise.

Ohio University's Department of Communication Sciences and Disorders has made this test available online for research purposes only (non-commercial use).
