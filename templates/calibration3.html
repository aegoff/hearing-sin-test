Troubleshooting Tips

  • Double check that your microphone has not been muted by your device, browser, or headphones.
  • Double check your headphones have a built in mic or access to your device's mic.
  • Make sure you have not disabled microphone permissions for your browser on your device.
  • Be sure to "refresh" after making any changes.
